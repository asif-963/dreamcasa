Welcome to DreamCasa

Looking for an affordable getaway surrounded by tea mountain views? DreamCasa offers cozy balcony rooms where you can enjoy the scenic beauty of Anachal. We provide homemade hot and spicy meals, along with a delightful campfire experience to complete your stay.

In addition to comfortable accommodations, we offer convenient services like local sightseeing taxis, including pickups and drop-offs from Cochin Airport, Allepey, and Thekkady. Various vehicle options are available to suit your budget and preferences.

Enjoy a peaceful garden, free private parking, and complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property. Our 24-hour front desk and full-day security ensure a comfortable and safe experience. DreamCasa features an a la carte or continental breakfast, perfect for starting your day with a taste of local flavors.
