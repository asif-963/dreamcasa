About The Homestay

Affordable Tea Mountain View with balcony rooms. We provide hot and spicy homemade food, campfire, and local sightseeing taxi service from Cochin Airport to Allepey and Thekkady. Pickup and drop services available for all types of vehicles at budget-friendly rates. Garden Dreamcasa features accommodations in Anachal.

This property offers access to a terrace, free private parking, and free WiFi. The accommodation includes a 24-hour front desk and full-day security for guests. Towels and bed linen are provided in the bed and breakfast. The property offers tea mountain views. The bed and breakfast serves an à la carte or continental breakfast.
